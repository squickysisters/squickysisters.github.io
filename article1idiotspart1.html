Incompetent People

By Ella M and Jordan K

What is an Idiot?

An incompetent person is a person who has no common sence and is unable to function properly in our society. Being surrounded by these people can make your day very hard and dreadful. So in this article I will be sharing my top tips on how to work with and around incompetent people.

The first way to deal with incompetent people is to put yourself in their shoes, and think why they might be doing what they are doing. This can help you communicate with them because you are now on their level, but what about when their level is unreachable? For example if you are in a group project with 2 great workers and 1 incompetent person (IP), you can moniter the IP for a short amount of time to see what they are doing, hoping that their further actions will make sense, but in the chance that it doesn't, try to talk to them. Some things to ask them could be "Can you explain your thought process to me?", "You seem confused, do you need help? " or "Can you show me what part of the project you are working on?"
